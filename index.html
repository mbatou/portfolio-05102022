Project

One Take Care

Faagaru is a web and mobile application that allows patients or just ordinary people to create their own medical records in order to take advantage of all the benefits offered by the service; real-time care in case of an emergency or to make an appointment, location of health posts and private or public hospitals, real-time data management by the treating staff, additional modules to make the patient's circuit easier and simpler. On the side of the treating staff it is a real time management; updated data for more efficiency, automated processing of certain requests such as appointments etc... A management of pathologies and statistical data necessary for decision makers.
